About

Heya everyone! This is Ananya. I am an Irish-Indian. I've lived in Ireland for three years and then for fourteen years in India. I'm currently living in Dubai and completing my last two years at high school at the American School of Dubai. Just like any other kid, I'm also very curious, in search of new things, trying to discover the world and most importantly myself.

I hope to live in a world where people view things with more than one perspective. This is something Psychology, an interest that I'd like to pursue in the future. My heart beats for music and photography.

I want to use these passions of mine and a world of love, empathy and tolerance. Have fun exploring my website and getting to know me better!
